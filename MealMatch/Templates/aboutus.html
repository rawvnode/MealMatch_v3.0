Mission

Behind every great idea there is a story

MealMatch began as a discussion among friends. We were not happy with the amount of food wasted yearly. And decided to act against it!
Therefor we started to develop a webpage, that would not only try to reduce the amount of food waste. But also assist peoples economy.
We began our journey in late March 2017. With everyone starting from scratch in developing a webpage, we had to put in alot of heavy hours. The first drafts where not pretty...
But eventually after some though weeks, we created this!
The service now concsists of more then 8000 recipes, and it keeps growing! We want all of you to share your favorite recipes among each other!
Create your recipes, comment and rate on others, and let them know that you appreciate it!
On the 12th of May we launched our first beta of the service. All the user input we will get from this, will fuel our fires to keep on creating the most revolutionizing recipe search engine there is.
One love // The MM-crew


FAQ


Why are you so awesome?

The world wide web has revolutionized how people live their lives and simplified cooking by providing extensive recipe databases and search engines. Not sure what to eat today? Go to your favorite recipe site and look try something from the toplist. But what if you have leftovers at home or found something cheap on your local supermarket and you don't know what you can cook with it?


I found a recipe that is wrong/inappropiate, what should I do?

Just press the flag icon and leave a comment on what's wrong. Our crew will take a look and hopefully make it right. The Flag can be found underneath the recipes picture!


How do I search with a pantry?

By making an account on MealMatch. You will be able to make searches with your own pantry. This will allow MealMatch to make even better recipe matching with what you got at home. Don't have and account? Why not create one? SIGN UP!


What's the cost for this service?

MealMatch is a free to use service, but of course, donations are always appreciated. Donations will be invested in perfecting the search algorithm and services we are developing for you!
