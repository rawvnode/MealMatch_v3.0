Mission

Behind every great idea there is a story

The world wide web has revolutionized how people live their lives and simplified cooking by providing extensive recipe databases and search engines. Not sure what to eat today? Go to your favorite recipe site and look try something from the toplist. But what if you have leftovers at home or found something cheap on your local supermarket and you don't know what you can cook with it?

MealMatch aims to challenge the perception of ingredients as subordinated to a recipe. Instead of looking for a particular recipe and then get the recipes you need for it, go to your pantry or your supermarket and use our matching algorithm to find out what you can cook. Ask not what you need to do for your recipe - ask what you can do for your ingredients!
